MEPR100+ PASSPORT SCANNER

The MEPR100+ Passport Scanner Software is an advanced solution designed to streamline identity verification and document processing. This software is specifically tailored for businesses and organizations requiring fast, accurate, and secure passport scanning and data extraction. The MEPR100+ software works seamlessly with passport scanners to capture and extract information from passports, visas, and other travel documents. It uses OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology to accurately read MRZ (Machine Readable Zone) codes, capturing critical data such as names, passport numbers, and expiration dates within seconds. With a user-friendly interface, the software ensures ease of use while maintaining high-speed performance.
